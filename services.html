Our Services

Practical data capability solutions designed for organisations

Hennotes Data Dossier provides a range of data capability and analytics services designed to help organisations move from fragmented reporting to confident, data-informed decision making.

Our services are modular and flexible. Organisations can engage with a single service or adopt a structured progression based on their current maturity, goals, and constraints.

How Organisations Typically Engage

Most engagements begin with understanding how data is currently used across the organisation.

From there, we help teams strengthen foundational skills, improve analytics delivery, and support leadership with decision-focused insights.

Some organisations work with us on short-term initiatives, while others engage over longer periods to build sustainable internal capability.

How Our Services Fit Together

HDD services are designed to work as an integrated system rather than isolated offerings.

A data capability assessment informs training priorities. Training supports analytics enablement. Analytics feeds decision support. Advisory ensures continuous improvement and alignment with business goals.

This approach allows organisations to progress at a pace that matches their capacity and readiness.

Who Our Services Are For

Our services support organisations at different stages of their data journey.

  • Leadership teams seeking clarity, confidence, and alignment in decision making.
  • Operational teams looking to improve reporting, analysis, and performance tracking.
  • Data and analytics teams aiming to deliver more impactful, business-aligned insights.
